Discover the Enchanting Wildlife of Sri Lanka

Welcome to a land where Huge forests, mist-shrouded mountains,
and sun-kissed beaches converge to create a haven for biodiversity.
Sri Lanka, despite its modest size, stands tall as one of the world’s top five biodiversity hotspots.
Let’s delve into the captivating tapestry of flora and
fauna that graces this island gem.

1. Our goal

At Wild Life Wonders, our goal is to provide an unforgettable wildlife safari experience in the heart of Sri Lanka, while contributing to the conservation of our precious Wild Life.

We dedicated to offer our guests the best experience, immersing them in the diverse flora and fauna that Sri Lanka has to offer. Our expert guides, with their extensive knowledge and passion for wildlife, ensure that every safari is a unique and memorable journey.
In addition to our safari tours, we also offer a range of other activities designed to deepen your connection with the natural world.
From bird-watching walks to night-time jungle treks, we provide opportunities to encounter wildlife in its most authentic form. Our commitment to conservation extends beyond our tours. We actively participate in local conservation efforts and contribute a portion of our profits to wildlife preservation initiatives.
We believe that by protecting our wildlife, we are preserving our future. At Wild Life Wonders, we don’t just show you the beauty of Sri Lanka’s wildlife - we invite you to be a part of its preservation.
Join us on this incredible journey, and together, let’s make a difference for the wild life of Sri Lanka.

2. Our Commitment to Conservation

At Wild Life Wonders, we believe in responsible tourism. We are committed to
preserving the natural beauty of Sri Lanka and its diverse wildlife. Here’s how we contribute to conservation


  • Support Local Conservation Projects:
    A portion of our proceeds goes directly towards supporting local conservation efforts.
  • Educate and Inform:
    Our guides provide valuable information about the wildlife and their habitats, promoting awareness and understanding among our guests.
  • Minimize Impact:
    We adhere to strict guidelines to minimize our impact on the environment and the wildlife.
  • Conservation Efforts:
    By choosing us, you’re directly contributing to local conservation efforts.
  • Sustainable Practices:
    We follow eco-friendly practices in all our operations to minimize our impact on the environment.
